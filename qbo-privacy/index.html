Semper Ibi Logistics QBO Internal App Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Semper Ibi Logistics QuickBooks Online internal-use application used to connect authorized company workflows to QuickBooks Online for accounting review, drafting, and approval-gated operations.

Information We Access

The application may access QuickBooks Online accounting data that is authorized by the account owner or administrator, including chart of accounts data, company information, reports, transactions, and related accounting records needed for internal business operations.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for legitimate internal business purposes, including accounting review, workflow support, proposal preparation, reconciliation, and approval-gated bookkeeping operations.

Sharing of Information

Information accessed through this application is not sold. It is used only by authorized internal users, contractors, or service providers supporting Semper Ibi Logistics business operations, subject to appropriate confidentiality and security expectations.

Data Storage and Security

The application uses local and controlled business systems to store configuration files, access tokens, audit records, and workflow artifacts. Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to limit unauthorized access.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for internal accounting operations, legal compliance, audit support, or operational continuity.

Your Choices

Access to QuickBooks Online data is based on authorization by the relevant QuickBooks Online company administrator. Access can be revoked by removing the application authorization or by disabling internal use of the application.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to SemperIbiLogistics@gmail.com.
